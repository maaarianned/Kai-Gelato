ABOUT US

Kai's Gelato is a newly opened ice cream shop in the Philippines. We specialize in Italian-style gelato and feature a wide range of flavors from the classics like Vanilla and Strawberry to the unique like Pistachio and Hazelnut & Cookies. Our gelato is crafted daily in-house using the freshest ingredients, allowing us to provide an unforgettable experience with each bite. We also offer an array of toppings and mix-ins to further customize your order. Come and indulge in the delightful world of flavors at Kai's Gelato!
